Overview

Stripe Connect is a service Stripe offers to facilitate payments in Brightcove Beacon. To learn more about Stripe's accounts, see Creating a Stripe Account document.

Workflow

For Beacon Web App

  1. Login to your Beacon Web App.
  2. Click Settings, then, My Account.
  3. Click Manage Subscriptions.
  4. Notice how you can decide which subscription fits you better. When you select it, click subscribe.
    5. Note: When you click subscribe, a window for Review Purchase will pop up. Be sure to confirm everything is okay.
  5. It immediately takes you to the checkout stripe page. In this example, it only shows two payment methods. However, you can modify which methods you prefer. To learn more about this, see Payment method integration options.

For Smart Tv (Lg and Samsung)

Login to Beacon.

There are three ways to pay in Smart TV's:

  1. First way: When you sign in to the login, you will receive a pop-up message asking if you want to subscribe immediately.
  2. Second way: You can go to My Account, then Manage Subscriptions.

    Select the subscription, and accept the terms and conditions:

    Notice how it sends you to a QR code. Click it with your cellphone and fill in the data.

  3. Third way: It is through the details page. You choose an asset. Then, click Subscribe

    It sends you select your subscription

    4. Notice how it sends you to a QR code when you select your subscription. Click it with your cellphone and fill in the data.

  4. Your Payment if confirmed.
