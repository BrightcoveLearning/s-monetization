Overview

Stripe Connect is a service Stripe offers to facilitate payments in Brightcove Beacon. To learn more about Stripe's accounts, see Creating a Stripe Account document.

Workflow

In Beacon Web App

  1. Login to your Beacon Web App.
  2. Click Settings, then, My Account.
  3. Click Manage Subscriptions.
  4. Notice how you can decide which subscription fits you better. When you select it, click subscribe.
    5. Note: When you click subscribe, a window for Review Purchase will pop up. Be sure to confirm everything is okay.
  5. It immediately takes you to the checkout stripe page. In this example, it only shows two payment methods. However, you can modify which methods you prefer. To learn more about this, see Payment method integration options.
